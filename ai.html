My Artificial Intelligence (AI) / LLM Policy

Last Updated: June 20, 2026

At the time of this writing, I do not use generative AI tools (LLMs) to research, ideate, create copy/content, write, summarize, synthesize, create images, or otherwise "chat" about anything.

Except for the automatic creation of captions or sound cleanup in tools like Riverside, I do not use AI even in tools where such features are offered.

Please contact me if you have any questions.

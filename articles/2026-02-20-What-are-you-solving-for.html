What are you actually solving for?

February 20, 2026

Before you rush to implement a new tactic, ask:

  1. What business problem will this solve, and how will I know I made the right decision?
  2. Am I reacting based on a STATE (fear, overwhelm, scarcity, fomo) or from a place of neutrality?
  3. Will the output of this decision (strategy/tactic/tool) in any way betray my values, my vision, or my voice?

DO NOT move forward until you can honestly answer these questions and find alignment between your answers and the step you want to take.

Remember: In uncertain times, the SMARTEST lawyers move forward with decisiveness rooted in intention, not fear.
